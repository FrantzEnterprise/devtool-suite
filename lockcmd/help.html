❓ LockCMD Help Guide

Plain-English guide for every tool in the Locksmith Command Center. Each tool has a simple step-by-step example.

📞 Dispatch & Quotes

Call Triage & Dispatch

Turn a phone call into a ready-to-use job ticket in seconds.

📞

What it does: You pick the call type, property type, address, and urgency. It creates a ticket number you can text to the customer.

Example: Customer calls — locked out of their house at 123 Main St. You select "Lockout" + "Residential" + "Emergency". Hit Generate Ticket. It gives you ticket DSP-ABC123. Tap "Send via SMS" to text the customer the ticket right away.

Rekey Quote

Quick parts + labor estimate for rekey and master key jobs.

💰

What it does: Enter the number of cylinders, key type, brand, and whether it's high security. It calculates parts + labor and shows the total.

Example: Customer with 5 Schlage cylinders wants a master key system. You enter 5, select "Master key system", Schlage, No high security. It returns: $35 parts + $200 labor = $235 total.

Commercial Bid Builder

Generate a bid for multi-door commercial jobs.

📄

What it does: Enter the number of entry doors, panic hardware units, storage doors, and key system type. It builds a complete bid with parts and labor.

Example: An office needs 3 entry doors, 2 panic bars, and 6 closets all on a grand master key. Enter those numbers, select "Grand master key". It returns a full bid breakdown.

🚗 Automotive

Car Lockout Dispatch

Get tool recommendations and a dispatch sheet fast.

🚙

What it does: Enter year, make, model, location, and lockout type. It generates a dispatch sheet with suggested tools and an SMS link.

Example: 2020 Toyota Camry at 456 Oak Ave, standard lockout. Select year range 2016-2020. It gives you: wedge set, long reach tool, air wedge — and a dispatch SMS to send.

Key & Fob Quote

Estimate replacement cost for keys, transponders, fobs, and smart keys.

🔑

What it does: Pick the service type (basic key, transponder, fob, smart key, or combo), quantity, year, and make. It calculates per-unit and total price.

Example: Customer needs 2 remote fobs for a 2022 Honda Accord. Select "Remote fob — program", qty 2. It shows $75 each = $150 total including programming.

Ignition Service Guide

Diagnose common ignition issues and get repair guidance.

🔧

What it does: Pick the symptom — key won't turn, key stuck, no start, loose switch, or broken key. It shows likely causes and recommended action.

Example: "Key won't turn at all" — result says try jiggling wheel and spare key. If no luck, the lock cylinder likely needs replacement. Recommend on-site service.

🔒 Safe Service

Safe Service Intake

Document every safe job — brand, type, issue, age, and notes.

🔐

What it does: Select safe type, issue, brand, age, and add notes. Generates a reference number (SAFE-XXXX) for tracking. Works for all makes, eras, and types — antique, modern, GSA, everything.

Example: Customer has an antique floor safe, dial spins freely, unknown brand. Select "Floor/wall safe", "Dial spins freely", age "Antique". Add notes: "Sargent & Greenleaf dial". Hit Generate Intake. Get reference SAFE-ABC123.

Safe Service Pricing

Starting estimates for every safe service you offer.

💰

What it does: Pick the service, access difficulty, and safe class. It returns a starting estimate. Final price varies by brand, era, mechanism, and on-site assessment.

Example: Non-destructive opening, moderate access, commercial TL-rated safe. Starting estimate: ~$450. Note says "Contact for exact quote."

Services covered: Opening (non-destructive & destructive), combo changes (dial & digital), dial/keypad replacement, lock repair, digital upgrades, antique restoration, GSA service, warranty repairs, relocker resets.
