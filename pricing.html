❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work? ▼
Monthly subscription. Cancel anytime — no contracts, no hidden fees. Your saved data stays in your browser and works even if you cancel.
Is it really privacy-first? ▼
Yes. All data stays in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to our servers. The only network requests are DNS/WHOIS lookups you explicitly trigger in the IP Network tool.
Can I use the tools without an account? ▼
Absolutely. The Free tier requires no signup. All 11 tools work at full functionality. Pro and Team only add save/export/sharing features.
What's the difference between Pro and Team? ▼
Pro gives you up to 10 saved scenarios per tool and export capabilities. Team adds unlimited saves, shared collections, and 5 seats for your team.
Can I try Pro before subscribing? ▼
Click the tier badge at the top of any tool to cycle through Free/Pro/Team and see what each unlocks. No payment info needed to explore.
