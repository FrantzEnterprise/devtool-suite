❓ TowCMD Help Guide Tow Command Center — guided intake flows for towing & roadside assistance

TowCMD helps your customers request a tow or roadside help without a long phone call. They answer a few simple questions, then their phone opens a text or email already addressed to you with everything filled in. They must tap "Send" for you to receive the message. TowCMD cannot send anything by itself — it only opens their phone's messaging or email app with the draft ready to go.

⚙️ Setup Guide — First Time

The first time you open LockCMD on a new device, you'll see the setup wizard. Complete all 7 steps — your settings are stored in your browser and won't reset unless you clear them.

🔧
  1. Welcome — click "Get Started" to begin, or "Skip" to use default settings (PIN will be 0000)
  2. Create a PIN — 4-6 digits. This locks the settings drawer so customers can't change your configuration. Don't forget this PIN.
  3. Business name — appears in the top bar so customers know who they're contacting
  4. Which services do you offer? — toggle the tabs you provide. Customers will only see tabs you leave on. Turn off anything you don't do.
  5. Brand colors — match LockCMD to your website. Pick accent, secondary, and background colors.
  6. Logo & background image — optional. Paste a URL to your logo or a background image.
  7. Your contact info — your phone number and email address. These are used in the SMS and email links so the customer's message goes directly to you.
  8. Finish — LockCMD launches with your settings applied.
Example: Bob only does residential and safe work. In step 4 he turns off Commercial and Automotive. He sets his phone to 9165344900 and email to bob@example.com. Now customers only see Residential and Safe tabs, and any text or email they send goes directly to Bob's phone and email.

🔒 Changing Settings Later

After setup, the ⚙️ gear icon appears in the top bar. Click it, enter your PIN, and the settings panel gives you full control:

  • Show or hide any service tab
  • Fine-tune which services you offer — inside each category, check or uncheck individual services so customers only see options you actually provide
  • Change business name, phone, or email
  • Change colors, logo, background
  • Change your PIN
  • Export settings to a .json file for backup
  • Import settings from a previous backup
  • Reset All — wipes everything back to defaults (including PIN, re-shows setup wizard)

📱 How To Send — Important

⚠️ LockCMD does not send messages automatically. It opens your phone's text or email app with a draft that's already addressed to the towing and recovery with all the details filled in. You must tap the "Send" button for the towing and recovery to receive your request.

At the end of any flow, you'll see two buttons:

  • 📱 Send via Text — opens your messaging app. The towing and recovery's number is already filled in as the recipient. The message body contains all the information you entered. Tap Send.
  • 📧 Send via Email — opens your email app. The towing and recovery's email is already in the "To" field. The subject and body contain your request. Tap Send.
  • 📋 Copy — copies the message text to your clipboard so you can paste it anywhere
Example: Sarah fills out the Residential flow. She taps "Send via Text." Her phone opens a new message to Bob's number already containing her request. She sees the Send button in her messaging app and taps it. Bob gets the text with Sarah's name, address, and what she needs. If Sarah just closes the app, Bob never gets the message.

🔄 Need More Than One Service?

If a customer needs multiple things done (for example a rekey and a safe combo change), they can:

  1. Complete the first flow (Residential → Rekey)
  2. Tap the "Also need another service?" button for the next category
  3. Complete the second flow
  4. Send each message separately — or use the Copy button to combine them into one

🏠 Light-Duty Tow Flow

For cars, SUVs, and motorcycles needing towing, jump starts, flat tires, or winch-outs.

🔑
  1. Pick the situation — locked out, rekey, repair, new install, or key duplication (only what the towing and recovery offers)
  2. Pick the lock type — deadbolt, knob, handle set, smart lock, or not sure
  3. Set urgency — emergency (locked out now), today, or schedule
  4. Enter your name
  5. Enter your phone number
  6. Enter the service address

🏢 Roadside Flow

For flat tires, jump starts, lockouts, fuel delivery, and tire repairs on the go.

🏢
  1. Pick what you need
  2. Pick the location type
  3. Enter vehicle make & model
  4. Enter your name
  5. Enter your phone number
  6. Enter exact location

🚗 Heavy-Duty Flow

For semis, box trucks, equipment hauling, buses, RVs, and farm equipment.

🚗
  1. Pick the vehicle type
  2. Loaded or empty?
  3. Pick what happened
  4. Enter company name
  5. Enter contact phone
  6. Enter current location

🔒

❓ Tips

  • Each flow takes about 30 seconds to complete
  • Use ← Back if you make a mistake
  • Tap "Start Over" at the end to redo a flow
  • Switch between tabs anytime — each flow is independent
  • If you need multiple services, complete one flow, then tap the next category button that appears, then send each message
