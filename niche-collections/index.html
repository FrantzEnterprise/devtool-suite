Why This Works

For the business owner:
  • Customers tell you what they need instantly — no phone tag, no "I'll call you back"
  • Structured intake means you get clean, consistent info every time
  • Self-hosted on your own GitHub Pages or website — zero recurring cost
  • PIN-protected settings so customers can't change your config
  • Export/import settings for backup between devices

For the customer:
  • No download, no account creation, no login required
  • Answers 5-6 simple questions — takes 30 seconds
  • Press one button — their phone opens a pre-written text or email
  • They just tap "Send" and the business gets their request

Technical:
  • Single HTML file — open it on any phone, tablet, or desktop browser
  • No backend, no database, no API calls — fully self-contained
  • Settings stored in browser localStorage (no account needed)
  • Works as a standalone link OR embeddable on any website
  • SMS and email links use the business's actual contact info
